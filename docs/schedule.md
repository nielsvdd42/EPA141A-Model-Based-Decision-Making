## Assessment

The course is assessed entirely through the **group project** (groups of 4 students). There is no written exam.

| Component | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Part 1: Model-based analysis and policy advice | 70 % |
| Part 2: Political reflection on the climate change debate | 30 % |

See [Final Project](project/index.md) for full instructions and rubric.

Assignments (1–8) are **not individually graded** but build the analytical foundation for the project. Not completing them seriously will likely result in a poor project grade.
